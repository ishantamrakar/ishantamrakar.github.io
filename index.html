About

I'm a PhD student at UMD advised by Yiannis Aloimonos and Cornelia Fermüller. My research sits at the intersection of computer vision, event cameras, and vision-language models for robotic perception.

I'm interested in building systems that understand the world the way robots need to: reliably, in real time, and under conditions where traditional cameras struggle. I also TA for introductory CS courses and enjoy making algorithms approachable through interactive demos.
